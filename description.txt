BuddyFinder

BuddyFinder is a real-time, heads-up, augmented reality system which displays the relative position of other systems and waypoints.

BuddyFinder can be integrated into goggles, helmet visors, hats, etc. and is intended for use in remote outdoor activities.

The range of BuddyFinder is approximately 2km in ideal conditions.

BuddyFinder can prevent seperation of groups, outdoorsmen getting lost, and aide in course avalanche searche.s
